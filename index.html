How to Buy Aurelius (SN37) Tokens

A step-by-step guide to purchasing Aurelius tokens

1

Acquire TAO

Purchase TAO (Bittensor's native token) from a centralized exchange:

  • Coinbase
  • Binance
  • Kraken

You will need TAO to purchase Aurelius tokens.

2

Set Up a Bittensor Wallet

Transfer your TAO to a self-custody wallet that supports Bittensor:

  • Talisman Wallet (Recommended) — Browser extension with Ledger hardware wallet support
  • Other Bittensor-compatible wallets

Withdraw your TAO from the exchange to your wallet address.

3

Buy Aurelius (SN37) Tokens

You have two options for purchasing Aurelius tokens. We recommend DCA for larger purchases to minimize slippage.

Option AOne-time purchase on Taostats

  • Go to Taostats.io — Subnet 37
  • Connect your Talisman wallet
  • Use the swap interface to exchange TAO for Aurelius (Alpha) tokens
  • You can stake directly to the Aurelius validator during purchase

Option BDollar-Cost Average (DCA) via Mentat Minds

  • Go to MentatMinds.com
  • Set up recurring purchases to DCA into Aurelius over time
  • This spreads your entry across multiple price points

Important: DCA Staking Note

If you use MentatMinds for DCA, your tokens will initially be staked to a random validator. After your DCA period completes, proceed to Step 4 to restake to the Aurelius validator.

4

Stake to the Aurelius Validator

Ensure your Aurelius tokens are staked to the official Aurelius validator to maximize rewards:

  • Go to Taostats.io — Subnet 37
  • Connect your wallet and navigate to your staking positions
  • If staked elsewhere, unstake and restake to the Aurelius validator

Aurelius Validator Address (Hotkey):

5CSrYw5nGquFeZKL1Py8H3vgqcEh2v9pzDaFnrCFySG5m5AY

Verify: Always confirm you're staking to the correct validator by checking the address matches exactly.

Yield & Compounding: Any staking rewards you earn will be automatically restaked (auto-compounded), growing your position over time without manual intervention.
